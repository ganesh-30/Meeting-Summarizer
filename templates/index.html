How to transcribe your audio to text?

Step 1
Upload Your Audio File
Drag and drop your audio file, or select files from your device. No file size limitation means you can upload lengthy recordings, podcasts, or any other audio content without limits.
Step 2
AI Processing Begins
Advanced automatic speech recognition technology analyzes your audio in real-time. Our AI powered system processes multiple languages, identifies different speakers, and captures spoken content with high accuracy.
Step 3
Review and Export
Receive your transcript with speaker labels and timestamps for easy navigation. Download your accurate transcripts as TXT, PDF, DOCX, SRT format for subtitles, or VTT files.
